Question:  Why is AV7 not affiliated with any particular denomination?

Answer:  The New Authorized Version Foundation is a private, not for profit organization registered in the State of Washington (UBI# 602-600-332) and established for the purpose of administering the copyright of The AV7 Bible  ~  The New Authorized Version in Present-Day English.

The Foundation's mission is to facilitate the broadest possible distribution and readership of the AV7 Bible and to maintain its unique presentation concepts, including its emphasis on literal translation accuracy and on making the Bible easier to read and understand by focusing on its most essential truths.

As a "statement of faith," the Foundation holds the view that anything less than the entire Word of God is too abbreviated a doctrine, and anything more than the Word of God is too much. The Foundation does not embrace or reject the doctrine of any particular family of believers; but rather simply encourages everyone to read and enjoy all of God's Word and especially those passages that AV7 features as "the most essential truth in the Bible."

It is our hope and prayer that the AV7 presentation will be an effective way for believers to share their faith with others and that it will be a catalyst for many to make the decision to place their faith in God.
