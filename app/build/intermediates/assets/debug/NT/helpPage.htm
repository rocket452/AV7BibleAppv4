How to Use the AV7 Bible

Most functions are intended to be intuitive. The following is to provide a brief overview of most functions:

  • Tap the AV7 logo in the upper left of each screen to go to the home page.
  • Tap "The Invitation Bible" title in the upper right to go to the Table of Contents page.
  • Scroll up and down on each page to view the contents of each page.
  • Tap the "<" symbol in the bottom left corner to go back to the previous page.
  • Scroll to the bottom of each page to see additional options when applicable.
  • Tap the blue "Share this free book" button on the home page to send an email message to invite others to enjoy free AV7 Bible website.
  • Tap the blue "get a printed copy" button on the home page to purchase order a printged copy of The AV7 Bible from either the publisher, Communication Architects, or from Amazon.com.
  • Tap any button on the Table of Contents page to view the each corresponding book or additional information page. Some buttons will open to a "placeholder" page until the corresponding information is updated.
  • Additional "help" information will be added here as may be needed.
