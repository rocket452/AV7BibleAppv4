Question:  Why is there a difference between believing  in  God ... and believing God?

Answer:  Everybody believes  in  God because the fact that God exists is intuitively known within every human spirit.

Of course, atheists and fools deny the existence of God,  and agnostics are just skeptics who claim that it is not possible to know whether God exists or not because they have not yet had a personal experience with God.

However, simple logic and common sense prove to the unclouded rational mind beyond any shadow of doubt that the vastness of the infinite universe and the infinite beauty and infinite complexity of creation could not possibly have come into existence by random chance from nothing.
