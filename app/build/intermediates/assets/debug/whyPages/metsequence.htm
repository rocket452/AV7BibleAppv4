Question:  Why are search results for the Most Essential Truth (MET #001-#249) not always in numerical sequence?

Answer:  The 249 Most Essential Truth passages featured in The AV7 Bible are selected from the words of declarative instruction and declarations of universal truth that were actually spoken out of the mouth of the Word of God as found in the records of John,  Matthew,  Mark,  Luke,  and The Revelation.

Some of these declarative statements are recorded in only one book,  and some are recorded in two or more books.  The sequence in which these statements are recorded is sometimes different from one record to another.  Therefore, The AV7 Bible prepared a "harmony" of these individual records in order to combine them into a single, composite presentation and position essentially identical verses and passages in a contiguous arrangement.  This harmonization of the various accounts into one cohesive account produced the resulting sequence numbers for these 249 Most Essential Truths.
